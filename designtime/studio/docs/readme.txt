Introduction

  TIBCO BusinessEvents is used to detect complex patterns within the 
  real-time flow of simple events. BusinessEvents can help you to 
  detect and understand unusual activity, recognize trends and 
  advantageous situations, and monitor for specific forms of 
  problematic activity.
